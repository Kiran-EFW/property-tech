# PropTech Platform — Product Vision Document

## 1. Executive Summary

A MahaRERA-licensed, technology-enabled real estate investment platform focused on a single micro-market in Mumbai. The platform connects early-stage real estate projects with investors through a network of on-ground field agents who provide verified, curated deal flow.

**Core thesis**: In a market plagued by information asymmetry, aggressive sales tactics, and trust deficits, a hyper-local, curated, trust-first platform beats broad national platforms like Square Yards, 99acres, and MagicBricks — especially for early-stage investment opportunities.

**What we are NOT**: We are not a listing portal. We are not a classifieds site. We are not building VR tours or AI chatbots. We are a curated investment platform with boots on the ground and a tech layer that amplifies trust and operational efficiency.

### Key Differentiators

| Dimension | National Platforms (Square Yards, 99acres) | Our Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Coverage | 25+ cities, thousands of projects | One micro-market, deep expertise |
| Curation | List everything that pays | Only RERA-verified, team-vetted projects |
| Trust | RERA number displayed, no independent verification | On-ground due diligence reports, site visit videos, construction tracking |
| Sales approach | Aggressive call bombardment | Relationship-based, consultative, WhatsApp-first |
| Agent quality | Inconsistent across 5,000+ agents | Small, trained, accountable team |
| Investor focus | Primarily end-use buyers | Early-stage investors seeking returns |
| Post-investment | Abandoned after commission earned | Construction progress tracking, exit support |
| Cost transparency | Shows BSP only, hides total cost | All-inclusive cost calculator (base + GST + stamp duty + registration + maintenance) |

## 3. Problem Statement

### For Investors

1. **Information asymmetry**: No reliable source for which early-stage projects are worth investing in. Builders and brokers both have misaligned incentives.
2. **Trust deficit**: Fear of fraud, project delays, builder defaults. 2-5 year average delays historically. Examples: Jaypee, Amrapali, Unitech, HDIL.
3. **Hidden costs**: GST (5%), stamp duty (5-6%), registration (1%), brokerage (1-2%), maintenance during construction — total 10-15% above quoted price, rarely disclosed upfront.
4. **No post-investment support**: Once the commission is earned, investors are abandoned. No construction progress tracking, no exit support.
5. **Aggressive sales**: Square Yards' #1 complaint is call bombardment. Investors want information, not pressure.
6. **NRI-specific pain**: Cannot visit property, need POA, difficult to verify remotely, tax complications (DTAA), rental management from abroad.

### For Builders/Developers (Early-Stage Projects)

1. **Lead quality**: Portal leads from 99acres/MagicBricks are high volume, low quality.
2. **Channel partner management**: Managing hundreds of CPs, tracking commissions, handling disputes.
3. **Early-stage capital needs**: Need investors and buyers for initial phases to fund construction.
4. **Trust building**: Newer/smaller builders struggle to attract investors without brand recognition.

### For Field Agents

1. **No unified platform**: Agents juggle WhatsApp, Excel, builder portals, and manual processes.
2. **Commission tracking**: Payment delays, disputes, lack of transparency.
3. **Lead management**: No systematic follow-up tools for independent agents.
4. **RERA compliance burden**: Need to maintain registrations, display numbers, keep records.

## 6. Regulatory Framework

### MahaRERA Compliance (Critical — Non-Negotiable)

**Our Status**: We hold a MahaRERA license. This is our regulatory foundation.

#### Platform Registration

| Requirement | Status | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Register as RE Agent entity with MahaRERA | Have license | Maintain and renew before 5-year expiry |
| Registration fee | INR 50,000 + GST (entity) | Paid |
| Display RERA number on platform | Required | Display prominently on all pages, communications |
| Only list RERA-registered projects | Mandatory | Build verification workflow |
| Use carpet area (not super built-up) | Mandatory | Enforce in all listings |
| Maintain transaction records | Mandatory | Built into platform database |

#### Advertising Rules

- Every project listing MUST display the MahaRERA registration number
- QR codes linking to MahaRERA project page required in all advertisements
- Cannot advertise unregistered (pre-RERA) projects — absolute prohibition
- All images must be marked "artistic impression" if computer-generated
- Standard disclaimers required on all marketing materials

#### Penalties for Non-Compliance

| Violation | Penalty |
|---|---|
| Operating without registration | INR 10,000/day + up to 5% of property cost |
| Advertising unregistered project | Up to 10% of project cost |
| Providing false information | Up to 5% of project cost |
| Non-compliance with orders | Up to 1 year imprisonment |

#### RERA Verification Workflow

Since MahaRERA provides no public API, we must implement:

1. **Manual verification**: Team verifies every project against MahaRERA's search portal before listing
2. **Required fields**: RERA number mandatory for all project listings — no exceptions
3. **Periodic re-verification**: Monthly check that listed projects' registrations remain active
4. **Builder document collection**: Collect and verify IOD, CC, title documents, escrow account details
5. **Future goal**: Pursue formal MoU with MahaRERA for data access

### Tax & Financial Regulations

| Area | Requirement |
|---|---|
| GST on brokerage | 18% GST on commission income |
| TDS on brokerage | Builder deducts 5% TDS (Section 194H) |
| TDS on property sale | Buyer deducts 1% TDS if >INR 50L (Section 194-IA) |
| GST on under-construction | 5% (no ITC); 1% for affordable housing |
| PMLA | KYC compliance for all transactions |
| FEMA | NRI investment compliance — NRE/NRO account rules, repatriation limits |

### SEBI SM-REIT (If Fractional Ownership)

If we ever add fractional ownership features:
- Must register as SM-REIT with SEBI
- Minimum INR 50 Cr scheme size
- Investment manager net worth INR 20 Cr
- Mandatory listing on stock exchanges
- 95%+ distribution of net cash flow to unit holders
- This is a future consideration, NOT current scope

### Core User Journeys

#### Journey 1: Investor Discovers and Invests

```
1. Discovery
   Investor finds platform via Google (SEO), referral, NRI community, or social media.

2. Browse Curated Projects
   Views RERA-verified projects in the micro-market. Each project has:
   - RERA registration details with verification badge
   - On-ground due diligence report (title, approvals, builder track record)
   - Site visit photos/videos from field agents
   - Pricing with full cost breakdown (base + GST + stamp duty + registration)
   - Payment plan options (CLP, DPP, flexi)
   - Area infrastructure analysis (metro, roads, schools, hospitals)
   - Builder's past project delivery record
   - Comparable project pricing in the area

3. Express Interest
   Investor clicks WhatsApp CTA or submits interest form.
   Assigned a dedicated field agent within minutes.

4. Consultation (WhatsApp + Call)
   Agent understands requirements: budget, timeline, investment vs end-use, risk appetite.
   Suggests 2-3 projects that match.

5. Site Visit (Physical)
   Agent conducts on-site tour.
   Post-visit: Detailed feedback captured, follow-up within 24 hours.

6. Due Diligence Support
   Platform provides:
   - Title verification report
   - RERA compliance check
   - Builder financial health indicators
   - Area price trend analysis
   - Total cost calculator (all-inclusive)

7. Booking & Transaction
   - Booking amount coordination with builder (direct to builder escrow)
   - Agreement for Sale coordination with builder
   - Stamp duty and registration guidance

8. Post-Investment Updates
   - Construction progress updates (quarterly, with photos from field agents)
   - Payment milestone tracking (CLP schedule)
   - Market value tracking (area price trends)
```

#### Journey 2: Field Agent Manages Deals

```
1. Onboarding
   Agent registers on dashboard with RERA number, PAN, GST.
   Platform verifies RERA registration against MahaRERA.
   Agent completes product training (project details, compliance).

2. Receives Leads
   Leads assigned based on geography, expertise, performance.
   WhatsApp alert for new leads.
   Must make first contact within 5 minutes.

3. Manages Pipeline
   Dashboard shows: today's tasks, overdue follow-ups, upcoming site visits.
   Click-to-call and click-to-WhatsApp from lead cards.
   Lead status updates (new → contacted → site_visit → negotiation → booking → won/lost).

4. Conducts Site Visits
   Marks visit in dashboard with photos.
   Fills structured feedback form.
   Duration and outcome logged.

5. Closes Deals
   Views cost sheets and inventory from dashboard.
   Coordinates booking with builder.
   Tracks booking confirmation and agreement status.

6. Earns Commissions
   Transparent commission structure visible in dashboard.
   Earnings, pending, paid, TDS — all tracked.
   Commission milestone tracking (booking → agreement → payment).

7. Performance Dashboard
   Personal KPIs: leads, contacts, visits, bookings, conversion rates.
   Tier status and lead allocation priority.
```

#### Journey 3: NRI Investor (Remote)

```
1. Discovery via NRI community channels (UAE, Singapore, US, UK)
2. Browse projects with full due diligence reports online
3. WhatsApp consultation with assigned agent
4. Agent-recorded video walkthroughs shared via WhatsApp
5. Remote due diligence (all reports digital)
6. POA guidance for document execution
7. FEMA-compliant payment channel support (NRE/NRO/remittance)
8. Remote booking and agreement coordination
9. Construction progress updates via dashboard
10. Exit/resale support with tax advisory (DTAA, TDS, repatriation)
```

## 10. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Supply First (Month 1-3)

**Objective**: Get 5-10 RERA-registered early-stage projects on the platform.

1. **Builder outreach**: Leverage existing MahaRERA license and relationships. Target Tier 1-2 builders with active projects in the chosen micro-market.
2. **On-ground due diligence**: Visit every project. Verify RERA, title, approvals, construction status. Create due diligence reports.
3. **Content creation**: Site visit photos/videos (agent-recorded), area analysis reports.
4. **Agent recruitment**: Onboard 3-5 experienced, RERA-registered agents from the micro-market.

### Phase 2: Demand Generation (Month 3-6)

**Objective**: Generate first 100 investor leads.

1. **SEO/Content marketing**: "Best projects in [micro-market]", "[micro-market] investment guide", "[micro-market] price trends" — own the search for the micro-market. Programmatic SEO pages per locality.
2. **Social media**: Facebook/Instagram click-to-WhatsApp ads targeting Mumbai HNIs and NRIs in UAE/Singapore.
3. **NRI outreach**: Partner with NRI community groups, cultural associations in UAE and Singapore. Webinars on "Investing in Mumbai real estate from abroad."
4. **Referral program**: Existing investors refer others. Incentive: Cash/credit on successful referral booking.
5. **WhatsApp community**: Build a curated WhatsApp group for micro-market investment insights. Weekly market updates, new project alerts.

### Phase 3: Transaction & Trust (Month 6-12)

**Objective**: Close first 20 transactions. Build reputation.

1. **Close deals end-to-end**: Full support from interest → site visit → booking → agreement.
2. **Post-investment updates**: Monthly construction progress updates for all investors. This creates the trust flywheel — happy investors refer others.
3. **Collect testimonials**: Video testimonials from early investors.

### Key Technical Requirements

1. **Multi-role RBAC**: One API serves 2 apps with 4 roles. Middleware extracts role from JWT, handlers check permissions. Same `/leads` endpoint returns different data for agent (my leads) vs admin (all leads).
2. **WhatsApp Business API**: Template messages for lead nurture, site visit confirmation. Two-way conversation logging via BSP webhooks.
3. **Lead deduplication**: Phone number normalization (+91, 0-prefix, spaces, country codes). First-touch attribution with 90-day expiry.
4. **Audit trail**: Every lead status change, commission action logged to immutable events table with actor, action, timestamp. RERA compliance + dispute resolution.
5. **API versioning**: URL prefix `/v1/`. Frontend apps deploy independently — can't break old clients.
6. **Presigned uploads**: Photos/documents go directly to R2, not through API server. Size/type constraints enforced.
7. **i18n on web**: `@nuxtjs/i18n` with subpath routing (`/en/`, `/hi/`, `/mr/`). UI labels translated, project content stays English.

## 12. Design Language

### Design Principles

| Principle | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Trust over delight** | Clean, structured layouts. No playful animations, no gradients, no glassmorphism. Looks like a bank, not a food delivery app |
| **Data density done right** | Show numbers prominently — prices, areas, returns, costs. Don't hide behind "click to reveal" |
| **Hierarchy through typography** | Font weight and size guide the eye, not colors everywhere |
| **Whitespace as trust signal** | Generous spacing signals quality. Cramped layouts feel like 99acres |
| **Mobile-native patterns** | Bottom navigation, large tap targets, thumb-reachable actions. 70%+ Indian web traffic is mobile |

### Visual System

| Element | Choice | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary color** | Deep navy (`#1a2b4a` range) | Universal trust color — banks, insurance, investment platforms |
| **Accent color** | Muted teal (`#2a9d8f`) | Modern, premium, not cliché gold |
| **Success** | Green (`#16a34a`) | RERA verified, payment confirmed |
| **Warning** | Amber (`#d97706`) | Payment due, document pending |
| **Error** | Red (`#dc2626`) | Failed verification, overdue |
| **Neutrals** | Slate grays (`slate-50` through `slate-900`) | Warmer feel than pure gray |
| **Background** | White (`#ffffff`) with `slate-50` sections | Clean, readable in any lighting |
| **Dark mode** | Not building | Indian RE audience skews 35-55, expects light interfaces |

### Typography

| Choice | Value |
|---|---|
| **Heading + Body font** | Inter (variable weight) |
| **Devanagari font** | Noto Sans Devanagari (for Hindi + Marathi) |
| **Font stack** | `Inter, Noto Sans Devanagari, sans-serif` |
| **Devanagari line height** | `1.7-1.8` (taller than Latin `1.5` to accommodate ascenders/descenders) |
| **Number display** | Indian numbering format: `₹1,25,00,000` (lakhs/crores, never Western comma grouping) |

### Design Style

**"Elevated Flat"** — Clean surfaces with subtle depth. No glassmorphism, claymorphism, or liquid glass.

| Element | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Cards | White background, `shadow-sm`, `rounded-xl`, 1px `slate-200` border |
| Depth | Shadows only — `shadow-sm` for cards, `shadow-md` for modals |
| Accent | Solid fills, not gradients |
| Hover | Darken by one shade, not glow or blur |
| Animations | Minimal — 150ms ease transitions only |
| Icons | Lucide (ships with Nuxt UI) |
| Illustrations | None. Use real project photos |

### Design References

Indian investment apps users already trust:
- **Zerodha (Kite)**: Flat, minimal, data-dense, green/red for up/down
- **Groww**: Clean flat cards, subtle shadows, bold typography
- **Razorpay Dashboard**: White, blue accent, flat cards, data tables
